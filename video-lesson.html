The Simple Past Tense

Learn to describe things you did yesterday.

Lesson Notes

In this video, we cover phrases that will help communicate with your grandma.

  • WERUDINI-ni (-ni).
  • Example: "I went" is weriduni or ከይደ (keyde) depending on dialect context, but root applications often end in -ku for direct actions.
  • Make sure to practice the pronunciation of the harder guttural sounds mentioned at the 4:12 mark in the video!
