1. The Verb "to be" and Subject Pronouns

The verb “to be” in the present tense is conjugated as follows.

Person Singular English Plural English
3 m.እዩ
('əyu)		he isእዮም
('əyom)		they are
f.እያ
('əya)		she isእየን
('əyän)		they are
2 m.ኢኻ
('ikha)		you areኢኹም
('ikhum)		you are
f.ኢኺ
('ikhi)		you areኢኽን
('ikhən)		you are
1 c.እየ
('əyä)		I amኢና
('ina)		we are

This verb is used for relations of identity and qualification (as with adjectives).

Examples (Sentences with እዩ):
  • እቲ ወረቐት ጻዕዳ እዩ።
    'əti wäräqät ts'a'əda 'əyu.
    The paper is white.
  • እታ ኵዕሶ ክባብ እያ።
    'əta kʷ'əsä kəbab 'əya.
    The ball is round.
  • እቶም ሰብኡት ሓያላት እዮም።
    'ətom säb'ut ḥayalat 'əyom.
    The men are strong.
  • እተን ቈልዑ ንኣሽቱ እየን።
    'ətän q'äl'u nə'ashtu 'əyän.
    The children are small.

3. Negative of the Verb "to be"

The present tense of the negative of “to be” is as follows:

Person Singular English Plural English
3 m.ኣይኰነን
('aykonän)		he is notኣይኰኑን
('aykonun)		they are not
f.ኣይኰነትን
('aykonätn)		she is notኣይኰናን
('aykonan)		they are not
2 m.ኣይኰንካን
('aykonkan)		you are notኣይኰንኩምን
('aykonkumn)		you are not
f.ኣይኰንክን
('aykonkən)		you are notኣይኰንክንን
('aykonkənn)		you are not
1 c.ኣይኰንኩን
('aykonkun)		I am notኣይኰንናን
('aykonnan)		we are not
Examples:
  • እዚ መጽሓፍ ዓብዪ ኣይኰነን።
    'əzi mäts'ḥaf 'abyi 'aykonän.
    This book is not large.
  • እዚዶ ርሳስ እዩ፧ ኣይኰነን፡ ብርዒ እዩ።
    'əzido rəsas 'əyu? 'aykonän, bər'i 'əyu.
    Is this a pencil? No, it is a pen.

4. Possessive Pronouns

The possessive pronouns that are independent, or stand as separate words, are as follows:

Person Singular English Plural English
3 m.ናቱ
(natu)		hisናቶም
(natom)		their, theirs
f.ናታ
(nata)		her, hersናተን
(natän)		their, theirs
2 m.ናትካ
(natka)		your, yoursናትኩም
(natkum)		your, yours
f.ናትኪ
(natki)		your, yoursናትክን
(natkən)		your, yours
1 c.ናተይ
(natäy)		my, mineናትና
(natna)		our, ours
Examples:
  • ናተይ መጽሓፍ ኣብዚ ኣሎ።
    natäy mäts'ḥaf 'abzi 'alo.
    My book is here.
  • መጽሓፈይ ኣብዚ ኣሎ።
    mäts'ḥafäy 'abzi 'alo.
    My book is here.
  • ናትካ ድዩ፧ ናተይ እዩ።
    natka dəyu? natäy 'əyu.
    Is it yours? —It's mine.
  • ናትካ ድዩ፧ ናተይ ኣይኰነን።
    natka dəyu? natäy 'aykonän.
    Is it yours? —It isn't mine.
  • or ኣይናተይን እዩ።
    'aynatäyn 'əyu.
    It is not mine.
