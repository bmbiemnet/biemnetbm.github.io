Lesson 1: Basic Greetings

Master how to correctly greet males and females in Tigrinya.

The Golden Rule of Greetings

In Tigrinya, the way you say "How are you?" changes depending on whether you are speaking to a male, a female, or a group of people. This is one of the most important foundations to master as a beginner!

English Meaning Tigrinya Script Pronunciation
How are you? (To a male) ከመይ ኣሎኻ፧
Kemey alokha?
How are you? (To a female) ከመይ ኣሎኺ፧
Kemey alokhi?
Very well, thank you. ሰናይ፣ የቐንየለይ
Senay, yeqenyeley.

See it in a Conversation

Here is what a standard greeting exchange looks like when passing a male friend on the street in Asmara:

ከመይ ኣሎኻ፧
How are you?
kemey alokha?
ሰናይ፣ የቐንየለይ
Very well, thank you.
senay, yeqenyeley
